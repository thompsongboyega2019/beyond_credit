Whistleblowing

  • The purpose of whistleblowing is to alert authorities to unethical behavior and illegal infractions, enabling prompt action to rectify the issue. Unethical practices may encompass:
  • Financial malpractices, impropriety, or fraud;
  • Non-compliance with legal or regulatory obligations;
  • Actions endangering health, safety, or the environment;
  • Criminal activities;
  • Unethical conduct or behavior.
  • We strongly encourage you to report any such activities to the bank via the dedicated channels:
  • Email:
  • All communications will be handled confidentially

Protection Of Whistleblowers:

At all times, the organization is committed to safeguarding whistleblowers who disclose unethical practices. Measures in place for the safety of whistleblowers include:

  • - Ensuring confidentiality and sensitivity in handling disclosures.
  • - Providing the option for whistleblowers to remain anonymous; however, disclosing their identities may enhance the reliability of their reports.
  • - Only disclosing the identity of the whistleblower when mandatory for investigation or prompt action, or when the whistleblower consents to being identified.
  • - Shielding whistleblowers from any form of harm or reprisal originating from the organization.

By encouraging a culture of transparency and accountability, this whistleblowing policy aims to uphold the organization's values and objectives while ensuring compliance with legal and ethical standards. All employees and customers are encouraged to report any instances of unethical behavior without fear of retaliation.
