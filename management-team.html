Meet Our Senior Management Team

Jude Njugo

CEO, Beyond Credit Ltd

Brings over 25 years of experience in micro lending, working with SMEs, and has worked at the IFC (World Bank), KPMG/Andersen, and African Capital Alliance.

Education:

MBA, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). B.Sc. (Accounting) University of Lagos. Fellow, Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria. Microfinance Certified Professional (MCP).

Lanre Akinbo

CTO, Beyond Credit Ltd

An entrepreneur and multidisciplinary professional, founder & CEO of Wizer Advisory, with extensive experience in developing process automation, strategy, and financial models.

Education:

MBA, Columbia University. MBA, London Business School. B.Sc. (Mathematics) University of Lagos. Associate, Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria.

Samuel Oladunni

COO, Beyond Credit Ltd

Bringing over 18 years of experience in Manufacturing, FMCG, and Microfinance, with extensive expertise in SME finance, Risk management, Procurement management, Project management, Administration, HRM and more.

Education:

MBA, LAUTECH. PGD, Project Management, Metropolitan School of Business and Management, UK. B.Tech (Biological Science), FUTA. Fellow, Institute of Management Consultant of Nigeria. Associate, Chartered Institute of Personnel Management of Nigeria. Microfinance Certified Member (MCIB) from Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria.
