Board Of Directors

Meet Our Board Of Directors

Beyond Credit Ltd is governed by a dedicated and experienced Board of Directors, committed to steering our organization towards success and sustainability. Comprising individuals from diverse backgrounds and industries, our board brings invaluable expertise and insight to guide strategic decision-making and ensure effective corporate governance.

Our Board of Directors is responsible for overseeing the strategic direction and performance of our organization. Committed to upholding the highest standards of corporate governance, our board ensures transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct in all our operations.

Adedotun Sulaiman

Chairman

A seasoned business professional with notable board positions in over 18 different companies, currently holding the position of Chairman at several organizations, including Cadbury Nigeria and ABSA Group Limited.

Education:

First-Class Honours degree in Business Administration from the University of Lagos. Fellow, Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (FCA)

Laoye Jaiyeola

Member

A visionary leader with over thirty-five years of banking experience, past CEO of Nigerian Economic Summit Group (NESG).

Education:

Degree in Economics at the University of Ife, now Obafemi Awolowo University. Master’s degree in Banking and Finance from the University of Lagos. Master’s of Business Administration from Bangor University, Wales, United Kingdom. Fellow of Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN).

Lolomari George

Member

A finance professional with over 20 years of experience in Financial Accounting, Audit, Finance Support, SAP, and Treasury Funding/Financing.

Education:

B.Sc. Accounting University of Lagos. Fellow, Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (FCA). Warton School of Business University of Pennsylvania

Funmi Oyetunji

Member

An accomplished professional with about 40 years of corporate experience, with roles in auditing, banking, and real estate development.

Education:

B.Sc. Accounting, University of Nigeria. Participated in several board development courses at Harvard, INSEAD, among others.

Olu Ogunmoyin

Member
